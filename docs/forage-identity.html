The mark

One bear, one idea: a forager

A bear gathers good things from the wild — the same thing the app does with links. Every mark shares the same geometry (circle ears, round head, a soft cream muzzle) so the family reads as one identity. The peeking forager is the primary mark: a bear rising over a trail, mid-discovery.
